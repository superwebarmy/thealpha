Privacy Policy for TV Remote

Last updated: 18 Nov, 2025

Thank you for using TV Remote (“the App”).
This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that no unnecessary or sensitive data is collected.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

TV Remote does NOT collect, store, or share any of the following:

Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)

Photos, videos, contacts, or files

Location data

Account or device identifiers

Any sensitive personal information

Your data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Information the App May Collect

To function properly, the App may access or use certain device features:

2.1. Local Network Access

The App may scan your local Wi-Fi network to:

Discover compatible Smart TVs

Connect and communicate with your TV

This process happens entirely on the device.
No network data is collected or transmitted to any server.

2.2. Device Information (Non-Personal)

The App may collect non-identifiable information such as:

App version

Device model (anonymous)

Crash logs

This is only for:

Improving performance

Fixing bugs

We use standard tools like Google Play Services / Firebase Crashlytics, which may handle anonymous diagnostics data.
No personal data is included.

3. How We Use Information

Any information used by the App is for:

Discovering and connecting to your TV

Improving the App’s stability and performance

Providing remote control functionality

We do not sell, rent, or share any data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services such as:

Google Play Services

Firebase Crashlytics (for crash reports)

These services may collect device-level diagnostic data for debugging but do not collect personal information from this App.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

No personal data is stored

No data is transmitted outside your device

Local network communication occurs only within your home network

6. Children’s Privacy

TV Remote does not target or collect data from children.
Since no personal information is collected, the App is safe for all age groups.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Changes will be posted within the App or on the app store listing.
